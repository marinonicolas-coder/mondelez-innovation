💡 Tech Solutions for E-commerce

Problem: Sugar reduction = bitter taste

Solution: Masks unwanted flavors without altering sensory profile

Mondelez Cases:

  • Oreo Thins "Zero Added Sugar"
  • Cadbury Dark 85% "Smooth"
  • Trident Zero "Clean Label"

🫐 VITISOL (Grape Seed Extract)

Problem: Consumers seek "functional snacks"

Solution: Natural debittered antioxidants for health claims

Mondelez Cases:

  • belVita "Antioxidant Boost"
  • Cadbury "Wellness Bar"
